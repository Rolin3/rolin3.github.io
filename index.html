🎮 About the Game

Tile Stack is a browser-based match-3 puzzle game inspired by the viral tile-matching mechanics you know and love. The goal is simple: tap open tiles to move them into your slots, collect three identical tiles to clear them, and empty the entire board before you run out of space.

Every level is carefully designed so that all tiles can be cleared, but the order you remove them matters. Tiles buried under others are locked until you free them from the top down. Plan your moves, use the tools wisely, and see how far you can go.

🧑‍🏫 How to Play

  1. Tap an open tile on the top of the stack to move it into your slots.
  2. Collect 3 identical tiles in a row to clear them automatically.
  3. Clear all tiles before your 7 slots fill up to win the level.
  4. Use Undo and Shuffle when you need a second chance.

🧠 Tips to Win

  • Always prioritize tiles that are blocking the most other tiles underneath.
  • Keep your slots balanced — don't stack too many different tile types.
  • Save your Shuffle for moments when you have no safe moves left.
  • Plan a few moves ahead; the bottom layers often hide the trickiest tiles.
